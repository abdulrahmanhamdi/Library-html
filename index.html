Library

Cumhuriyet University Library is an important source of information that serves students, faculty, and researchers. Its collection includes books, journals, theses, articles, electronic resources, and other information materials. The library provides guidance on accessing information, offers information skills training, and supports the scientific research process. Additionally, it offers study areas, computers, and technological facilities tailored to the needs of users. In this way, Cumhuriyet University Library contributes to the university's education and research mission.

About Library

Libraries are collections of resources organized for easy access and retrieval, serving various purposes like education, research, and leisure. They come in different types, including public, academic, and special libraries, each offering specific services tailored to their users. With the rise of digital technologies, libraries are evolving to provide digital access to vast amounts of information while also preserving traditional formats.
